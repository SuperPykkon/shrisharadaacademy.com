Who are we?

Shri Sharada Academy is a coaching centre based in Bangalore, India. We specialise in teaching commerce and management courses. We also provide tuitions for various college and school subjects. Our trainers are well-versed in the field and put all their efforts on improving students' knowledge on the respective subjects. We were established in 2015 with the aim of providing concept based teaching to students through our professional staffs. We also offer online courses. Our simplified learning materials enhance the students' chances of cracking an exam.
